# Gold and Silver Price Analysis vs. Geopolitical Risk
![Gold-Silver Price VS Geopolitical Risk](./Report//Pictures/dataset-cover.png)


This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the relationship between gold and silver prices and the Geopolitical Risk (GPRD) index from 1985 to 2025. It serves as an infographic to visually summarize key findings and insights derived from the data.

## Key Findings

### 1. Gold Price Trends
Gold has shown a powerful long-term upward trend, especially after the early 2000s, serving as a significant safe-haven asset.

### 2. Geopolitical Correlation
The analysis revealed a very weak direct correlation (0.0013) between daily gold price changes and the overall GPRD index. This suggests that while geopolitical events can cause volatility, other macroeconomic factors are more influential.

### 3. Silver's Behavior
Silver prices show a strong correlation (0.707) with gold, indicating that these two precious metals tend to move in similar patterns.

### 4. Forecasting Challenges
An LSTM deep learning model was used for price forecasting. While it performed well on historical data, its predictive accuracy was lower for recent years, highlighting the challenge of modeling unpredictable market events without external data like inflation or policy changes.

## Visualizations
- **Price Trend Chart:** Visualizes the long-term upward trend of gold prices.
- **Correlation Scatter Plot:** Demonstrates the weak relationship between GPRD and gold price changes.
- **Volatility Distribution Chart:** Shows that daily price changes follow a near-normal distribution.
- **LSTM Performance Chart:** Compares the model's accuracy on validation vs. test data.
